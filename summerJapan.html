Summer in Japan

Hot days and pleasent nights, with abundant festivals and revelry

The most exciting time of the year. Don’t hate Japanese summer because it’s too hot and humid. There are many seasonal events in Japan during summer so why don’t you get into some of very special summer activities?? Let’s have the most amazing summer experience in Japan!
